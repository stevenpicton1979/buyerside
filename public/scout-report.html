06 5–10 Year Suburb Outlook

Chelmer benefits from Cross River Rail's Dutton Park station (3km, operational 2026), the Centenary Motorway upgrade, and its position in the Brisbane 2032 Olympics transport corridor. Rezoning risk to this precinct is low — the suburb sits in a character residential overlay.

However, the flood overlay creates structural headwinds for capital growth. Post-2022, similar flood-mapped properties in Chelmer and neighbouring Graceville have underperformed dry comparable streets by approximately 4–7% on a 3-year view. This is the key tension in this suburb.
